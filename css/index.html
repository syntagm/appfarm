About appFarm

We develop mobile applications and web apps for agriculture

We believe mobile applications and solutions for agriculture should be produced in close collaboration with primary producers and that the IP should stay close to where it was produced, in agriculture.

To this end we are creating fresh new apps and reinvesting the income in the production of more and better apps

Working apps?

We have a NFC/QR or Bar code enabled FruitFly monitor that was developed by a producer and has been tested extensively on an export oriented stonefruit farm.

The app accummulates trap-data in a password protected cloud-based database and all the historic data is immediately accessible in CSV or xLSX format.

Our mobile Asset manager app enables the tracking of any agri-asset including strategic stocks. It can facilitate better stock control routines between producer and CP field agents.
